## UnrealEngine | C++ | Gameplay & UI Programmer

A narrative multiplayer adventure set on a post-apocalyptic ocean world where humanity struggles to survive on floating settlements.

## My Contributions:

* Core programmer responsible for developing key gameplay systems such as Stealth & Chase and Carry mechanics, and for initiating the game’s unique asynchronous gameplay system. 
* Led UI development from concept to implementation, working closely with Art and Design to faithfully translate the artistic vision into the final product. 
* Created custom in-engine tools to improve workflow efficiency, including a JIRA-integrated bug reporting system.
